Clever Minds Development Center is committed in providing the community a well-organized environment that supports and nurtures young children’s intellectual, social, physical, and emotional development. CMDC would like to help children in adjusting to an environment outside the nuclear family. CMDC is devoted in providing a good place where parents can safely place their children.
